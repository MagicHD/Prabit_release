‌Privacy Policy

If you are a minor and find parts of this policy unclear, we encourage you to discuss it with a trusted adult before using Prabit. Please note that Prabit is not designed for individuals under 13 years old. If you are between 13 and 16 years of age, parental or guardian consent is required for you to use the app.

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect about you, how we use it, under what circumstances we share it, and the tools available for you to manage the data you share with us.



  1. ‌Information We Collect About You

    Prabit ("the App") gathers data that you directly provide or that is generated through your use of the app.

    1. ‌Information You Provide


      Account Information: When you create an account on Prabit, we require the following details to grant you access:

      • Your date of birth, email address, and username.

      • The email address of a parent or legal guardian if you are under 16.


        Additionally, you may choose to provide:


      • A profile picture.


        Content Information: By using the app, you can voluntarily share:


      • Photos and videos (“Content”) captured through the app or selected from your library. While account creation is possible without granting access to your camera or photo library, features like tracking progress through photos will be unavailable, as this is a key functionality of the app.

      • Your interactions with friends’ content, including captions, likes, and comments.

      • Your customized habits and associated data which include but are not limited to habit frequency, time and name

        Chat Data: Messages exchanged with friends through Prabit’s chat feature are collected.


        Communications with Prabit: When you contact us for support, report issues, or provide feedback, we may collect:

      • Your email address.

      • Details of your correspondence with us.

      • Photos you share with us voluntarily.

      • Copies of government-issued identification, when necessary for identity verification.

      • Other identifiers, such as phone numbers or additional information you choose to provide.

    2. ‌Information Automatically Collected


      Similar to other internet-based services, we collect information automatically when you use the app. This data includes:

      Technical and Connection Data:


      • Details such as your IP address, account creation date, last login, device type, operating system version, language preference, and browser information.

      • Unique identifiers such as user_id (created at account registration), device_id

        (generated when using a new device), and Amplitude ID.


        Usage Data: Including but not limited to Information about your app activity, including timestamps of content uploads, the number of posts, content interactions, deleted items, and associated habits.

        Geolocation Data: We infer your approximate location (e.g., country, city, or region) based on your IP address.

        Address Book Data: You can opt to allow Prabit access to your contact list to identify existing app users among your friends. If granted, we will securely hash the phone numbers in your contact list, save and compare them with our records, and return friend suggestions. Without this permission, personalized friend suggestions may still be generated using your existing

        in-app friends.


    3. ‌Information Provided by Third Parties


      We may also receive information about you from other users or external sources. For instance:


      • If someone you know uses the contact import feature, they may share your phone number with us, just as you might share theirs.

      • Friends who use the contact import option may result in mutual friend suggestions within the app.

      • Information about how you discovered our app, such as clicking on a social media advertisement, may also be provided by our partners.

      Partner Features: If we collaborate with third parties to provide new features, sharing relevant information with those partners may be necessary.

      Legal or Statutory Purposes: We may proactively share user data with authorities when legally obligated or in cases involving safety, fraud, or harmful content (e.g., child exploitation or terrorism).

    We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Data retention periods are aligned with applicable laws and guidance from regulatory authorities.



  7. ‌Controlling Your Information in the App


    At Prabit we have implemented features to ensure that you remain in control of your personal data.

    Notification Settings: You can manage which notifications you receive via the app’s settings.


    Blocking Users: You can block and unblock users at any time to control who interacts with your content.

    Content Visibility: You control the audience for your posts, deciding whether they are visible to friends, friends of friends, or the public. You can control this at any time in the content configuration.

    How other users use your Content is not under our control.


    Time Zone: When you create an account we determine your time zone for the purpose of providing you our App

    Deactivate Memories: This feature can be turned on or off to manage visibility of your previous content.

    Account Deletion: You can request account deletion via the contact form in the app’s settings. Deleted accounts undergo a 14-day recovery window, during which you can contact us to cancel the deletion after which all data is permanently erased, barring legal retention requirements. Note, information will not be deleted where Prabit has received a valid legal request requiring us to preserve your information.


    Consent: When we rely on your consent, you can withdraw your consent at any time via the contact form in the “settings” page of the App

    If your account is permanently suspended by us and you choose to delete your account, we will keep Account Creation Data related to your account to prevent permanently suspended users from creating new accounts.


  8. ‌Your Rights Over Your Information

    Privacy is a core human right. Regardless of where you live, Prabit ensures that you have the following rights concerning your personal information:

    • Right to Access and Obtain a Copy of Your Data: You can request details about the personal information we process about you, including the purposes, categories of data, recipients, and retention periods. Keep in mind that certain data like posted photos will already be deleted after a certain period of time automatically by us, so we won’t be able to share that data after deletion.

    • Right to Rectification: If your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request corrections or updates via the contact form in the “settings” page of the app.

    • Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten): Under specific circumstances, you may request the deletion of your data. Certain data may need to be retained due to legal obligations or to defend legal claims.

    • Right to Restrict Processing: In certain cases, such as when disputing the accuracy of your data, you may request a limitation on data processing.

    • ‌Right to Withdraw Consent: If processing is based on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing based on consent.‌

    • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing or other purposes, based on your specific situation.

    • Right to Data Portability: In certain situations, you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format to facilitate its transfer to another entity.

    • Post-Mortem Data Management: If you are a French user, you have the right to set directives on how your data is managed after your death.

    • Right to File Complaints: If you are located in the European Union, EFTA States, or the UK, you can raise concerns with your local supervisory authority or Prabit’s lead authority: Bundesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (BfDI).

    Some of these rights—such as rectification, deletion, and consent withdrawal—can be exercised directly through the contact form in the app’s settings. For additional assistance, contact our support team at support@prabit.tech.
